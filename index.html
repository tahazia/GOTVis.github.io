GOT Visualizations

Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama TV series based on the A Song of Ice and Fire (ASOIF) Series by George R. R. Martin. The series contains 7 books in total (5 of which are currently published) with an approximate average page count of 600 per book. The TV series based on these books spans currently across 8 seasons, with the final season premiering in 2019 -- after a 3 year gap between seasons 7 and 8. Due to the extensive amount of detail in the world designed by George R. R. Martin, and different storylines and character arcs playing out throughout the series; it becomes quite a hard task to be able to accurately remember everything that’s going on in the series. Usually, a few months before a new season’s premier, a large amount of recap videos are posted on youtube. These are usually anywhere from 5-20 minute long (sometimes even more) videos that recap most major plot events that have occured in the series so far. Sifting through these videos to find the relevant information is oftentimes difficult. Suppose you don’t care about what happens to Sansa Stark, you can’t really use filtration techniques on videos. Our target application for this project aims to provide an accurate and chronological visualisation of the character deaths in game of thrones. This can be broken down into a number of tasks (which can also later be appended to):

  1. Allow users to interactively see and filter through results of major house battles.
  2. Allow users to chronologically filter through events. In our case, the order follows from the episodes.
  3. Get different metrics based on a character.
